<p>Monthly working sessions for operators who want a sharp outside read on what to ship next — and a sparring partner between sessions when the call needs to be made quickly.</p>

<p><em>The pattern.</em> Each month opens with a 90-minute working session: stack review, what's stuck, the one lever that matters this month. Between sessions, async over Slack — me on call for the tactical decisions where a second opinion saves a week.</p>

<p>Three slots open at a time. Bias toward operators 18+ months into the work — the conversations are more useful when the foundations are real.</p>
