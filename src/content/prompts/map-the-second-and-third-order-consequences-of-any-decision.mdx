I can see the first-order consequences (what happens immediately). I need you to map the second-order (what happens because of what happens) and third-order (what happens because of THAT) consequences across every domain this decision touches.\n\nMy decision:\n[DESCRIBE THE DECISION AND THE FIRST-ORDER CONSEQUENCES YOU ALREADY SEE]\n\nDomains this affects:\n[LIST THE AREAS OF YOUR LIFE/BUSINESS THIS TOUCHES — e.g. \"revenue, team morale, personal health, market position, relationships, skills, reputation\"]\n\nFor each domain, trace the chain:\n\n**First Order** (0-3 months) → **Second Order** (3-12 months) → **Third Order** (1-3 years)\n\nMark each consequence as:\n- 🟢 Beneficial\n- 🔴 Harmful\n- 🟡 Uncertain (and what would resolve the uncertainty)\n\nThen identify:\n1. **Hidden traps** — Second or third-order consequences that are negative but invisible at first order\n2. **Hidden gifts** — Second or third-order consequences that are positive but not the reason you'd make this decision\n3. **Feedback loops** — Where a consequence at one level amplifies or dampens a consequence at another level\n4. **Cascade risks** — Where a single second-order consequence could trigger a chain of failures\n5. **Temporal reversibility** — Which consequences are reversible at each order? Where's the point of no return?\n\nEnd with: knowing all of this, does the decision still look the same? What would you change about HOW you execute it (not whether you do it) to maximise the hidden gifts and defuse the hidden traps?"
