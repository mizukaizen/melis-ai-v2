Streaming happened to music before video. Aggregation happened to travel before food delivery. Unbundling happened to newspapers before banking. I want to find what's about to happen to MY industry by finding the pattern match.\n\nMy industry:\n[DESCRIBE YOUR INDUSTRY AND ITS CURRENT STATE]\n\nCurrent tensions:\n[WHAT FEELS BROKEN OR ABOUT TO CHANGE — e.g. \"pricing is opaque\", \"incumbents are slow\", \"customers are underserved in X\", \"regulation is outdated\"]\n\n**STEP 1 — Pattern Extraction**\nLook at 10+ industries that have already gone through a major disruption. For each:\n- What was the pre-disruption state?\n- What was the catalyst?\n- What did the post-disruption landscape look like?\n- How long did the transition take?\n\n**STEP 2 — Analogy Matching**\nWhich 3-4 industry disruptions most closely match the current tensions in my industry? For each:\n- What's the structural similarity?\n- What's different (and does the difference help or hurt the analogy)?\n- What happened in Year 1, Year 3, Year 5 of that disruption?\n\n**STEP 3 — Timeline Projection**\nBased on the best analogies, project:\n- What happens to my industry in the next 12 months?\n- What's obvious in 3 years that almost nobody sees today?\n- Who are the winners and losers?\n- What new roles/companies/categories emerge?\n\n**STEP 4 — Action Extraction**\nGiven this projection:\n- What should I start building NOW (before the pattern becomes obvious)?\n- What should I STOP investing in (things that look solid but are in decline)?\n- What's the contrarian positioning that lets me ride this wave instead of being crushed by it?\n\nBe specific about the analogies. \"Like Uber for X\" is lazy.
