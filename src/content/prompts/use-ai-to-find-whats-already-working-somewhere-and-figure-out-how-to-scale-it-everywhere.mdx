Rwanda has better healthcare delivery than many wealthy nations. Estonia runs an entire government digitally. Singapore turned water scarcity into water abundance. The solutions exist — they just haven't travelled.\n\nThe problem I want to solve:\n[DESCRIBE THE PROBLEM — e.g. 'affordable housing in my city', 'youth unemployment in my region', 'food waste in restaurants', 'access to mental health support']\n\nWhere I am:\n[YOUR COUNTRY/CITY/CONTEXT]\n\n**STEP 1 — Global Solution Scan**\nSearch for places that have meaningfully solved or improved this problem:\n- Which countries, cities, or organisations have the best outcomes?\n- What specifically did they do?\n- What were the conditions that made it work there?\n- How long did it take to show results?\n- What's the evidence that it actually worked (not just good PR)?\n\nFind at least 5 examples across different contexts (developing/developed, different cultures, different scales).\n\n**STEP 2 — Extract the Mechanism**\nFor the top 3 solutions:\n- What's the core mechanism that makes it work?
