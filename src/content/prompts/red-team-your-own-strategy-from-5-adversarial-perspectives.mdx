Each persona wants to DESTROY my plan — their job is to find the fatal flaw.\n\nMy strategy:\n[DESCRIBE YOUR PLAN, BUSINESS, OR STRATEGY IN DETAIL]\n\n**PERSONA 1 — The Hostile Competitor**\nYou have unlimited resources and you want to crush this strategy.
