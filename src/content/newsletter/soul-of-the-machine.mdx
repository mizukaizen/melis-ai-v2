<p>I have been running an experiment on myself for the last fourteen days: every time I noticed my attention shift, I wrote down what it shifted to and what triggered it.</p>

<p>The findings are mostly embarrassing. The interesting part is the unembarrassing 8%: the shifts I am proud of, and what pulled me there.</p>
