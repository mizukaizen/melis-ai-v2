What is melis.ai?

A studio, not a content brand.

melis.ai is my product lab and AI education studio — where a decade of hands-on AI work gets packaged into courses, tools, and resources that non-technical people can actually use. If I haven't shipped it in real work, it doesn't make it in here.

The Mission

Help 1 billion people achieve at least one important goal using AI.

That's not a marketing line. It's the filter for every decision — what to build, what to teach, what to say no to. AI fluency shouldn't be a privilege reserved for engineers and enterprise teams.
